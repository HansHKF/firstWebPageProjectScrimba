Alnes

The Alnes Lighthouse is a historic lighthouse located on the north side of Godøy island in the municipality of Giske, Norway. It was established in 1853 to guide fishing boats safely to the harbor of the small fishing community of Alnes.
The lighthouse offers a magnificent view of the surrounding area, including the beautiful sea landscape. It is a popular destination for visitors who can enjoy the stunning views of the ocean, whether it's a sunny summer day or during a winter storm. The lighthouse is situated about 20 kilometers northwest of Ålesund and can be reached by car through three sub-sea tunnels. It is definitely worth a visit for those seeking a picturesque sight and a glimpse into Norway's maritime history.

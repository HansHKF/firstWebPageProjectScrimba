About us

Royal Tur Reiser

Royal Tur Reiser is a small tour company that was established in July 2018 with the aim of offering first-class travel experiences. We are dedicated to delivering comfort and quality through our services. We offer fixed tours to iconic destinations such as Alnes lighthouse and Trollstigen, so that our customers can experience Norway's natural beauty at its best. We tailor our services to meet your needs, and we offer pick-up and drop-off at destinations according to your wishes.
